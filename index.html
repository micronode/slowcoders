Slow Coders

The Slow Coders movement is dedicated to the pursuit of improved software design and implementation. The key tenants of Slow Coding are:

  • Thoughtful reasoning about features and architectural design (i.e. "You ain't gonna need it (YAGNI)")
  • Addressing "code smells" (technical debt) as a priority
  • Feature-driven deployments (as opposed to fixed deadlines)
